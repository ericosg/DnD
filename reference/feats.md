# All 5e Feats — Quick Reference

Some feats have prerequisites (noted in parentheses). The "Best For" column suggests which classes and builds benefit most, but any character can take any feat they qualify for.

---

## Combat Feats

| Feat | What It Does | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Alert** | +5 initiative, can't be surprised, hidden attackers don't get advantage on you | Assassin Rogues (Assassinate triggers on surprise), any character who wants to act first. Universally strong. |
| **Crossbow Expert** | No loading penalty, no disadvantage on ranged in melee, bonus action hand crossbow attack | Ranged Fighters, Rogues using hand crossbow builds, Rangers. The bonus action attack is a reliable way to fish for Sneak Attack. |
| **Defensive Duelist** (DEX 13+) | Use reaction to add proficiency bonus to AC against one melee attack | DEX melee characters (Rogues, Duelists). Competes with other reaction options like Uncanny Dodge or Shield spell. |
| **Dual Wielder** | +1 AC while dual wielding, can use non-light weapons, draw two weapons at once | Two-weapon fighting builds (Fighters, Rangers, Rogues). Lets you use rapiers (1d8) instead of shortswords (1d6). |
| **Fighting Initiate** | Learn one Fighting Style (e.g., Two-Weapon Fighting: add ability mod to off-hand damage) | Any non-Fighter who wants a Fighting Style. Popular with Rogues (Two-Weapon Fighting) and Rangers (Archery from a different angle). |
| **Great Weapon Master** (heavy weapons) | Bonus action attack on crit/kill, trade -5 to hit for +10 damage | STR Fighters, Barbarians, Paladins using greatswords/greataxes. One of the highest damage feats in the game. |
| **Gunner** | +1 DEX, firearm proficiency, no disadvantage on ranged in melee | Campaign-dependent (requires firearms). Good for any DEX character in a setting that uses them. |
| **Martial Adept** | Learn 2 Battle Master maneuvers, 1 superiority die (d6) | Fighters (especially non-Battle Masters who want a taste), but the single d6 is underwhelming. Better as a multiclass complement. |
| **Polearm Master** | Bonus action butt-end attack, opportunity attacks when enemies enter reach | STR Fighters, Paladins, and Barbarians using polearms. Often paired with Sentinel for a devastating combo. |
| **Sentinel** | Opportunity attack stops movement, can attack when allies are targeted, ignore Disengage | Frontline defenders (Fighters, Paladins, Barbarians). Excellent for battlefield control. Pairs with Polearm Master. |
| **Sharpshooter** | Ignore cover, no disadvantage at long range, trade -5 to hit for +10 damage on ranged | Ranged Fighters, Rangers, Rogues with longbow/crossbow builds. One of the highest damage feats for ranged characters. |
| **Shield Master** | Bonus action shove with shield, add shield AC to DEX saves | Sword-and-board Fighters, Paladins, Clerics. The shove can knock enemies prone for advantage on follow-up melee attacks. |
| **Tavern Brawler** | +1 STR or CON, unarmed strike 1d4, grapple as bonus action | Grapplers, Monks looking for bonus action grapple, or any STR character who wants unarmed options. Niche but fun. |
| **War Caster** (spellcasting) | Advantage on concentration, somatic components with full hands, spells as opportunity attacks | Any concentration caster, especially melee casters (Clerics, Paladins, Bladesingers, Eldritch Knights). Top-tier for gish builds. |

## Stealth & Exploration Feats

| Feat | What It Does | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Skulker** | Can hide when lightly obscured, missed ranged attacks don't reveal you, dim light doesn't impose Perception disadvantage | Rogues, Rangers, and any stealth-focused character. Strongest in dungeon or nighttime environments. |
| **Mobile** | +10 ft. speed, difficult terrain doesn't slow you when you Dash, no opportunity attacks from creatures you melee | Rogues (frees up Cunning Action), Monks, melee characters who need hit-and-run tactics. Universally useful. |
| **Dungeon Delver** | Advantage on finding traps/secret doors, resist trap damage, search for traps at normal pace | Rogues and any party scout in dungeon-heavy campaigns. Situational but powerful when it applies. |
| **Athlete** | +1 STR or DEX, standing from prone costs 5 ft., running jumps without extra movement, climbing doesn't cost extra | Grappler builds, anyone who gets knocked prone often, or characters who need the half-ASI. Minor but consistent benefits. |

## Skill & Social Feats

| Feat | What It Does | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Observant** | +1 WIS or INT, +5 passive Perception, +5 passive Investigation | Any WIS character (Rogues, Rangers, Clerics, Druids). Passive Perception of 20+ makes ambushes nearly impossible. |
| **Skill Expert** | +1 any stat, one new skill proficiency, one new Expertise | Rogues, Bards, and any character who wants to specialize. The flexible ASI and Expertise make this universally good. |
| **Skilled** | Gain 3 skill proficiencies | Bards, Rogues, or any character who wants broad utility. Best for characters who already have good ability scores. |
| **Prodigy** (half-elf, half-orc, or human) | +1 skill, +1 language, +1 tool, +1 Expertise | Humans, half-elves, and half-orcs who want Expertise outside of Rogue/Bard. Great for skill-monkey builds. |
| **Actor** | +1 CHA, advantage on Deception to impersonate, mimic voices | Bards, Warlocks, CHA-based characters, or anyone playing an infiltrator/spy concept. |
| **Keen Mind** | +1 INT, always know north/time, perfect recall of last month | Wizards or INT characters who want a small boost. Mechanically weak but great flavor for investigators or scholars. |
| **Linguist** | +1 INT, learn 3 languages, create ciphers | INT characters in intrigue-heavy or exploration campaigns. Very niche. |

## Toughness & Survival Feats

| Feat | What It Does | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Tough** | +2 HP per level (retroactive) | Any squishy character (Wizards, Sorcerers, Rogues) who keeps dropping. Simple and effective. |
| **Resilient** (pick an ability) | +1 to chosen ability, proficiency in that saving throw | Any character shoring up a weak save. Resilient (CON) for casters maintaining concentration, Resilient (WIS) for Fighters/Rogues against charm/fear. |
| **Lucky** | 3 luck points per day — reroll any d20 (yours or an enemy's) | Literally everyone. Widely considered one of the strongest feats in 5e. Three "nope, reroll that" per day. Some DMs ban it. |
| **Durable** | +1 CON, minimum HP from Hit Dice during short rest = 2x CON mod | Characters who rely on short rests (Fighters, Warlocks, Monks). Generally considered weak compared to Tough. |

## Magic & Spellcasting Feats

| Feat | What It Does | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Magic Initiate** | Learn 2 cantrips + 1 level-1 spell from any class's list | Non-casters who want utility (Find Familiar for advantage setup, Goodberry for healing). Great for Rogues, Fighters, Barbarians. |
| **Ritual Caster** (INT or WIS 13+) | Cast ritual spells from a book | Non-casters with 13+ INT or WIS who want utility spells without spell slots. Detect Magic, Identify, and Find Familiar are standouts. |
| **Shadow Touched** | +1 INT or WIS, learn Invisibility + 1 illusion/necromancy spell, cast each once per day free | Any character who wants once-per-day Invisibility. Popular with Rogues, Rangers, and any WIS/INT character. |
| **Fey Touched** | +1 INT or WIS, learn Misty Step + 1 divination/enchantment spell, cast each once per day free | Any character who wants a 30 ft. bonus action teleport. Universally excellent. Popular with Rogues, Rangers, Clerics, and anyone who needs repositioning. |
| **Eldritch Adept** (spellcasting or Pact Magic) | One Warlock invocation | Warlocks primarily. Casters who want Devil's Sight or other invocations. |
| **Metamagic Adept** (spellcasting) | 2 sorcery points, 2 Metamagic options | Sorcerers (more Metamagic options) or other casters who want Subtle Spell or Quickened Spell. |
| **Elemental Adept** (spellcasting) | Ignore resistance for one damage type, treat 1s as 2s | Casters who spam one damage type (Fireball Wizards, Lightning Sorcerers). Niche but impactful in the right build. |
| **Spell Sniper** (spellcasting) | Double spell range, ignore cover, learn a cantrip | Ranged casters, especially Warlocks using Eldritch Blast. Great for battlefield control casters. |
| **Telepathic** | +1 INT, WIS, or CHA, telepathy 60 ft., cast Detect Thoughts once per day | Intrigue/social characters, GOO Warlocks, or anyone who wants silent communication. Flavorful and moderately useful. |
| **Telekinetic** | +1 INT, WIS, or CHA, invisible Mage Hand, bonus action shove 5 ft. | Characters who don't use their bonus action often. The shove can break grapples or push enemies into hazards. |

## Racial / Niche Feats

| Feat | What It Does | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Healer** | Stabilize + heal 1d6+4+level with a healer's kit, once per creature per rest | Parties without a dedicated healer. Any character can become a competent field medic. Scales with level. |
| **Inspiring Leader** (CHA 13+) | Give temp HP to allies after a speech | CHA-based characters (Bards, Paladins, Sorcerers, Warlocks). Significant party-wide survivability boost. |
| **Charger** | Dash + bonus action attack with +5 damage or shove | Melee characters without good bonus action options. Generally considered weak. |
| **Grappler** (STR 13+) | Advantage on grappled creatures, pin them | STR-based grapplers (Fighters, Barbarians). Niche build. |
| **Heavy Armor Master** (heavy armor proficiency) | -3 to non-magical physical damage | Heavy armor Fighters, Paladins, Clerics. Strongest at low levels, diminishes as enemies deal more damage. |
| **Medium Armor Master** (medium armor proficiency) | +3 DEX to AC in medium armor, no stealth disadvantage | DEX-based characters in medium armor (Rangers, certain Clerics, multiclass builds). Solid if you have 16 DEX and medium armor. |
| **Heavily Armored** (medium armor proficiency) | +1 STR, heavy armor proficiency | Clerics or other medium-armor classes who want heavy armor. |
| **Lightly Armored** | +1 STR or DEX, light armor proficiency | Wizards, Sorcerers, or other classes without armor proficiency. Rarely needed due to multiclassing options. |
| **Moderately Armored** (light armor proficiency) | +1 STR or DEX, medium armor + shields | Rogues, Warlocks, or others who want better AC without multiclassing. |
| **Weapon Master** | +1 STR or DEX, 4 weapon proficiencies | Rarely needed — most martial characters already have what they need. Occasional use for specific multiclass or racial builds. |
| **Savage Attacker** | Reroll melee damage dice once per turn, take higher | Melee characters with large weapon dice (greatswords, greataxes). Generally considered weak since it only affects weapon dice, not bonus damage. |
| **Mage Slayer** | Reaction attack on casters in melee, advantage on saves vs. adjacent casters, impose disadvantage on concentration | Melee characters in campaigns with lots of enemy casters. Situational but devastating when it applies. |
| **Mounted Combatant** | Advantage on melee vs. smaller unmounted, redirect attacks from mount to you, mount takes no damage on DEX save success | Paladins (Find Steed), Cavalier Fighters, or any mounted build. Campaign-dependent. |

## Top Feats by Role

| Role | Top Feats |
|---|---|

| **Ranged Striker** (Archer Fighter, Ranger) | Sharpshooter, Crossbow Expert, Alert, Lucky |
| **Frontline Tank** (Fighter, Paladin, Barbarian) | Sentinel, Polearm Master, Great Weapon Master, Heavy Armor Master, Tough |
| **Skill Monkey** (Rogue, Bard) | Skill Expert, Observant, Lucky, Prodigy |
| **Full Caster** (Wizard, Sorcerer, Cleric, Druid) | War Caster, Resilient (CON), Lucky, Fey Touched, Shadow Touched |
| **Stealth/Infiltrator** | Alert, Skulker, Mobile, Shadow Touched, Fey Touched |
| **Support/Healer** (Cleric, Bard, Druid) | War Caster, Resilient (CON), Inspiring Leader, Healer, Lucky |
