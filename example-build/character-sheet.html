Fusetsu — Combat & Reference Cheat Sheet
Gloom Stalker Ranger — Print back-to-back with character sheet
Alert Feat — Always Active
+5 initiative (stacks with DEX +3, and WIS +3 at level 3 = +11). Can't be surprised — you always act on round 1, protecting Dread Ambusher. Hidden attackers don't get advantage against you.
Combat Turns
Ranged Turn — default
  1. Move up to 30 ft. (split before/after action)
  2. Action: Longbow → d20+5, 1d8+3 piercing
  3. Bonus Action: Nothing at Lvl 1. At Lvl 2+: cast/move Hunter's Mark (+1d6 per hit)
  4. Reaction: Opportunity attack with shortsword → d20+5, 1d6+3
Melee Turn — When They Get Close
  1. Drop or stow longbow (free interaction)
  2. Action: Shortsword → d20+5, 1d6+3
  3. Bonus Action: Off-hand shortsword → d20+5, 1d6 (no DEX mod to damage)
Ranged attacks within 5 ft. of a hostile have disadvantage — that's when you switch to swords.
Dread Ambusher Turn — Round 1 Only (Level 3+)
  1. Speed is 30 + 10 = 40 ft. this round
  2. Action: Longbow → d20+7 (Archery), 1d8+4 + Hunter's Mark 1d6
  3. Extra Attack (Dread Ambusher): Longbow → d20+7, 1d8+4 + 1d8 + Hunter's Mark 1d6
  4. At Level 5 with Extra Attack: three longbow shots on round 1
Gloom Stalker in Darkness (Level 3+)
  • In darkness, if enemies use darkvision to see: you are invisible to them
  • All your attacks have advantage (unseen attacker)
  • They can't target you with attacks requiring sight
  • You don't need to Hide — you're just invisible. Stand in the open.

Feat Details
Alert (Level 1)
+5 initiative. Can't be surprised. Hidden creatures don't get advantage on you. Protects Dread Ambusher from ever being wasted.
Sharpshooter (Level 8)
Choose per attack: -5 to hit, +10 damage. Ignore half/¾ cover. No disadvantage at long range (600 ft longbow, no penalty). The reckless all-or-nothing shot.
Key Rules to Remember
Ranged in melee: Disadvantage on ranged attacks when hostile creature within 5 ft. Switch to shortswords.
Weapon swap: One free object interaction per turn. Stow bow + draw one sword = free. Drawing second sword costs next turn's interaction. Ask DM.
Two-weapon fighting: Off-hand attack is a bonus action. No ability mod to off-hand damage (you don't have the fighting style for it).
Hunter's Mark: Bonus action to cast. Bonus action to move to new target. Lasts 1 hour (concentration). Competes with off-hand attack for bonus action.
Concentration: Only one concentration spell at a time. Taking damage → CON save (DC 10 or half damage, whichever higher) or lose the spell.
Opportunity attack: Reaction when creature leaves your melee reach. One melee attack. Shortsword, not longbow.
Cover: Half cover = +2 AC. Three-quarters = +5 AC. Full = can't be targeted. Sharpshooter ignores half and ¾.
Longbow range: Normal 150 ft, long 600 ft. Long range = disadvantage (unless Sharpshooter). No minimum distance.
Armor upgrade: Studded leather (45 gp) → AC 15. First purchase priority. Avoid medium armor — Stealth disadvantage ruins Gloom Stalker.
